About me:

I'm a physiotherapy student, certified sports massage therapist, and passionate mobility coach - blending science, soul, and tradition in every session. But my story began long before formal training... I grew up around healing..my great-grandmother was a massage therapist and osteopath, and she passed down a deep belief that touch can be a powerful form of care. Today, I honour that legacy by helping people move better, feel stronger, and reconnect with their bodies, through mobility, holistic massage, and mindful recovery practices. Based in London, but dreaming & building something that goes beyond borders From Puglia's orchards to barefoot rituals to movement labs in London - bridging nature, movement, and modern healing.

FAQ:

What makes your sessions different?

Each session is completely personalised. I combine sports massage, deep stretch, and mindful touch with an intuitive approach - so no two treatments ever feel the same. It's not just about recovery; it's about reconnection.

How long are the sessions?

Sessions are available from 30 to 120 minutes, depending on what your body and schedule need most. We'll decide together what's right for you.

Do I need to prepare anything before coming?

Just wear something comfortable and arrive with an open mind. You don't need to do anything special — I'll guide you through everything.

Is it only for athletes?

Not at all. BeKindBloom experiences are for everyone - whether you train hard, sit at a desk, or simply need a reset. The goal is to help your body feel lighter and your mind calmer.

Where are the sessions held?

I currently work from selected wellness spaces and studios in London, and also offer private sessions upon request. Details are shared once we connect.

How much does it cost?

Because every person blooms differently, sessions are priced individually based on duration, frequency, and specific needs. This keeps things fair, accessible, and fully tailored.

Can I book regular sessions or packages?

Absolutely. Many clients choose to book regular sessions to support recovery, flexibility, or emotional balance. Packages are available for returning clients.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes — you can gift someone a BeKindBloom experience, perfect for birthdays, wellness surprises, or just to say "you deserve a pause."
